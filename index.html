A History

Wartime

After wartime, the United States military set up sex trafficking rings for US soldiers’ enjoyment. Despite the euphemism “comfort women” given to the East and Southeast Asian women trafficked, these women were actually routinely raped. Furthermore, these setups were seen as necessary for the soldiers, perpetuating how Asian women were seen as just objects for sexual satisfaction.
